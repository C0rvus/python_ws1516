We used the data portal from the city of chicago and visualized the data for the purpose of obtaining answers on the following questions.

Which kind of crime is most common?
When do most crimes occure?
How many arrests have been carried out?
Where are most crimes committed?

Which kind of crime is most common?

The following stacked bar chart shows the most committed crimes for each year (Tip: Hover over the chart for detailed information). Consistently among the top are battery, theft, criminal damage and narcotics.

When do most crimes occure?

Surprisingly most crimes are not comitted after midnight, 5 to 6 am even marks the low point. Most crimes occur between 8 and 9 pm. The line chart also visualizes, that there are less crimes comitted over the years.

How many arrests have been carried out?

The pie chart illustrates, that after 2004 - the year most arrests were carried out (144.661) - the quantity of arrests has been decreasing every year. Only 78.833 arrests have been carried out in 2014.

Where are most crimes committed?

The geographical data allowed us to overlay a heatmap on Google Maps and to visualize the hot spots, where crimes were comitted.
