Crime Statistics

Statistics on crime in Chicago, which were comitted between 2001 and 2014.

About the project

We used the data portal from the city of chicago and visualized the data for the purpose of obtaining answers on the following questions.

Which kind of crime is most common?
When do most crimes occure?
How many arrests have been carried out?
What is the percentage of domestic violence?
Where are most crimes committed?
